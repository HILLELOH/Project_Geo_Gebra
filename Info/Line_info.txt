A Line extends infinitely in both directions through two defined points.

Representation:  y = mx + b
  m  =  slope  =  (y2 − y1) / (x2 − x1)
  b  =  y-intercept

Special cases:
  • Vertical line   →  x = c          (undefined slope)
  • Horizontal line →  y = c          (slope = 0)

Key distinction from Segment:
  A line has no endpoints — it continues forever in both directions.
  A segment is the bounded piece between two endpoints.

Properties:
  • Area    = 0
  • Length  = undefined (infinite)

Interaction:
  Click two points to define the line's direction.
  The line will extend beyond both points across the entire canvas.
