A Point is the most basic geometric object — a single location in 2D space.

Representation:  P(x, y)
  where x is the horizontal coordinate and y is the vertical coordinate
  on the Cartesian plane.

Properties:
  • Has no size, length, area, or volume
  • Identified by a unique label (A0, B0, …)
  • Serves as the building block for all other shapes

Interaction:
  • Click to place  •  Drag to move  •  Right-click to set exact coordinates
  • Left-click in the Algebra panel to select and highlight
