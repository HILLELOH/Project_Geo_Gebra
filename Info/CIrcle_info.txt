A Circle is the set of all points equidistant from a fixed center point.

Representation:  (x − a)² + (y − b)² = r²
  (a, b)  =  center coordinates
  r       =  radius (distance from center to any point on the circle)

Calculations:
  Area          =  π · r²
  Circumference =  2 · π · r
  π ≈ 3.14159265…

Properties:
  • Perfectly symmetric around its center
  • Every point on the boundary is exactly r units from the center

Interaction:
  Click to place the center, click again to set the radius.
  Drag the center point to move the circle.
  The radius is fixed at the distance you clicked.
