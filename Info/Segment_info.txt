A Line Segment is a finite straight line bounded by two endpoints.

Representation:  segment AB from A(x1, y1) to B(x2, y2)

Equation of the line through the segment:
  y = m(x − x1) + y1
  where  m = (y2 − y1) / (x2 − x1)

Length (Euclidean distance):
  |AB| = √[(x2 − x1)² + (y2 − y1)²]

Properties:
  • Area   = 0
  • Has two distinct endpoints
  • Finite length

Interaction:
  Click to set start point, click again to set end point.
  Drag either endpoint to reshape. Drag the segment itself to move it.
